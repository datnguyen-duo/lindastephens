Featured Work

Project Management Advisors (PMA)

PMA is a national real estate advisory firm providing consulting services as the owner’s representative, managing projects across a diversity of markets. PMA needed to refine their brand messaging for a new website. Following a brand review, we worked collaboratively with a core stakeholder group to identify key differentiators based on extensive client feedback. A fresh approach to messaging was created along with copy for the website and other marketing materials. On an ongoing basis, we craft thought leadership content for PMA leaders, conduct client interviews for strategic endorsements, provide LinkedIn training for staff and consult on a range of marketing initiatives.

"We initially hired Linda to help us refresh our messaging and develop copy for a new website project. This successful collaboration introduced us to Linda’s creativity and expertise in leveraging messaging and content to support PMA's strategic brand positioning efforts. On an ongoing basis, Linda now contributes to many of our marketing initiatives including generating proprietary content gathered through interviews of leaders from each of our regional offices and updating our marketing materials to better reflect the refreshed messaging. Linda’s down-to-earth perspective and depth of experience are invaluable to PMA’s marketing efforts."
-Jill Tarby, Vice President Marketing, PMA

Aurora Lighting Design

Aurora Lighting Design creates inspiring environments working directly for clients or as a specialty consultant to a larger architectural design team. Their website was dated, lacked cohesion and was ineffective at communicating the firm’s extensive experience and capabilities. Our creative team led the firm through a brand review and deep assessment process designed to bring clarity and refinement to their strategic positioning. This served as the foundation for a completely new website design including key messages and copy development.
